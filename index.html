A one of a kind, FPS gaming expo and festival for people of all ages, Experts and noobs alike. An exhibition of FPS games by some of the world's best developers will be on display. B.Y.O.C enouraged as available on site consoles may be restricted by demand.

2023.02.4(SAT) ~ 5(SUN)

@ Diamond Plaza II, Parklands, Nairobi

Launch

Welcoming of guests and introduction to attending developers/firms, and organizers

Forum

Discussion on the latest gaming tech, upcoming FPS games and general Q&A

Exhibition

Showcasing of the latest games by attending developers

Gaming competitions for registered participants and general gameplay for non-competing attendees

Award ceremony for competing participants, giveaways by organizers and free online party for a limited time.
