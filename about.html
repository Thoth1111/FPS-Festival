NAIROBI FPS GAMING FESTIVAL 2023

The FPS (First Person Shooter) festival is an annual event for lovers of FPS games on all devices (PC, Console or mobile). It brings together the best gamers in the nation as well as international game developers to showcase the very best in FPS games and provides an avenue for networking among gaming enthusiasts. If you are a gaming enthusiast or player looking to complete, this is the event for you. Gather your squad and link up for the baddest funnest shootout event of the year.

For more information about the event, please shoot us an email
fps-fest@godaddy.com

Nairobi FPS Festival 2023 Logo

The FPS logo was decided through an online logo competition in which participants were required to design a log that best suited an FPS gaming event. Held in December of 2022, the winner, Alwyn Androvelle was awarded 4 free tickets and a gaming console of his choice. The logo will remain the official symbol of Nairobi FPS festival until December of 2025.
